About Mentor Market:

Mentor Market connects aspiring developers with experienced coding mentors.

Learning to code can feel overwhelming — especially when you don’t know who to ask. Mentor Market was created to give you direct access to real developers who have already navigated the challenges you are facing.

Instead of generic tutorials or endless forums, you can speak to someone who understands your goals, your blockers, and your journey into tech.

Mentors share their expertise, set their availability and offer 1:1 guidance. Visitors can explore profiles, choose a time slot, and book a session with ease.

How Mentor Market works:

For Visitors

1. Browse mentor profiles and find someone who matches your goals.
2. View their available time slots.
3. Select a slot and submit your booking request.
4. Receive confirmation and get connected with your Mentor.

For Mentors

1. Sign up. 2. Create and submit your mentor profile.
3. Set your availability by choosing dates and time slots.
4. Review booking requests from visitors.
5. Confirm sessions, contact your Mentee and manage your bookings in one place.
